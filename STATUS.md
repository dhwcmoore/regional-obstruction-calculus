The disjoint case's branchwise/aggregate split survives fully into the compatible-coupled case, at least for this witness. Not a general theorem тАФ evidence on one witness family, not all possible ones. One instance of this finding is now also `coqchk`-verified independently of the Python machinery тАФ see ┬з1's `CoupledParallelCompatibility.v` row.

- 1000-case random-residue regression (`tests/test_random_residue_regression.py`).
- Coupled realisability diagnostics: independent generator, shared adjacent-overlap coupling, ordered inclusion-exclusion, Candidate 3b on both distinct- and repeated-support covers.
- Conflict-resolution trilemma classification table (`conflict_resolution_trilemma_probe.py`): checks `docs/design/CONFLICT_RESOLUTION_TRILEMMA.md`'s ┬з4 table over exact rationals тАФ `left_wins`/`right_wins`/`average`/`sum`/`erase` against agreement, idempotence, left/right fidelity, and symmetry. ## 3. Diagnostic witnesses, not general theorems

These establish that one specific candidate rule does or does not do something on one specific construction тАФ not a theorem about every rule in its class:

- **Independent generator** (`realisability_diagnostic.py`): too free тАФ surjective onto all of `C^1(N;Q)`.
- **Shared adjacent-overlap coupling** (`coupled_realisability_diagnostic.py`): rank drops but the image collapses entirely into `im(delta^0)` тАФ cohomological collapse.
- **Boolean proper-crossing diagnostic** (`boolean_crossing_diagnostic.py`): a deterministic, parameter-free rule that produces a non-degenerate residue outside `im(delta^0)` on one specific cover. Non-linear тАФ no rank or quotient to compute.
- **Ordered inclusion-exclusion** (`lattice_ie_diagnostic.py`): globally-indexed but the associator formula cancels exactly the shared terms тАФ full rank, disguised independence.
- **Candidate 3b, distinct-support cover** (`candidate_discipline_diagnostic.py`, `rocq/CandidateThreeBDistinctSupportClassification.v`): cover-inert тАФ the cover never lets two triple overlaps coincide, so nothing can be shared. Now machine-checked (┬з1) as a general fact about any pairwise-distinct-support assignment, not just the one concrete cover the diagnostic ran.
- **Candidate 3b, repeated-support cover** (`repeated_triple_support_diagnostic.py`, `rocq/RepeatedTripleSupportCandidate3b.v`): the first positive linear/rational witness тАФ `rank(B)=2`, neither full rank nor coboundary collapse. Also machine-checked in Rocq, which upgrades the *incidence condition and the induced-map facts* to proved status (┬з1) while the underlying claim тАФ that this is a structurally meaningful or unique coupling discipline тАФ remains diagnostic.

## 4. Not claimed

This repository does not claim:

- full presentation invariance (comparing two different coarse presentations of the same regional situation and concluding their obstruction verdicts agree, in general);
- a fully verified end-to-end implementation (the Rocq proofs are abstract or instantiated on the paper's own concrete numbers; they do not mechanise `finite_algebra.py` or `regional_composition.py` themselves тАФ see each `rocq/*.v` file's header comment for its exact scope);
- that every associator residue is structurally meaningful тАФ the independent generator (┬з3) is a counterexample to that on its own;
- that every coupled generator produces a nontrivial obstruction quotient тАФ most tried so far do not (┬з3);
- that Candidate 3b is the final, unique, or general coupling principle тАФ it is one rule, verified on one family of covers.
- that disjoint parallel composition preserves the *aggregate* (scalar) (A4) in general тАФ ┬з1's `A4_parallel_aggregate_can_fail_despite_branchwise` is a machine-checked witness where two certificate-disjoint branches each individually satisfy (A4) but their direct-sum composite's aggregate pairing cancels to exactly zero (branchwise preservation, proved unconditionally in ┬з1, is a genuinely different and weaker-to-state, stronger-to-hold claim than the aggregate);
- that coupled parallel composition preserves anything **in general** тАФ no preservation candidate exists for it, in either this repository or `veribound-fce`'s design documentation. ┬з2's shared-seam probe is evidence on 2 hand-picked *consistent-declaration* cases only, not a theorem, and says nothing at all about the *conflicting*-declaration case beyond "no composite exists to test." ┬з1's `CoupledParallelCompatibility.v` proves exactly that well-definedness fact тАФ agreement is necessary and sufficient for a glue to exist тАФ and nothing about (N0)/(A4)/(E0) preservation of one;
- that a conflicting shared seam has any principled resolution тАФ no merge, averaging, or branch-preference rule is proposed anywhere in this project; conflicting declarations are diagnosed (`interface_conflict`), not resolved;
- that compatible aggregate-A4 cancellation is *common*, *typical*, or *inevitable* тАФ ┬з2's Phase 5d found and verified that it is *possible*, on one witness family, not a statement about frequency across any wider class of witnesses this project has not built;
- that any conflict-resolution rule is correct, recommended, or endorsed тАФ ┬з1's `ConflictResolutionTrilemma.v` and ┬з2's probe classify seven candidate resolver shapes by what each sacrifices; none is chosen, and ┬з4 of the design doc states explicitly that `external_authority` is not even the same *kind* of object the other six are;
- that ┬з1's non-lossy lower bound (`ConflictResolutionLowerBound.v`) specifies a concrete diagnostic schema тАФ it characterises the abstract minimum (injectivity on `V * V`), not field names, types, or serialisation formats. No such schema is designed in either repository.
- that R13's bounded completeness (`ConflictDiagnosticCompleteness.v`) proves completeness for all possible fusion, policy, or coupled-composition systems тАФ it proves completeness for one small, explicitly bounded four-constructor fragment, chosen to match this project's own proved results and `veribound-fce`'s own applied vocabulary. A real system with, say, a partial-recovery diagnostic would need its own fragment;
- that R13 chooses a resolver, solves general coupled parallel composition, handles policy authority/downstream fusion/restriction/failure composition, or wires anything into `fce_check.py` тАФ see `docs/design/CONFLICT_DIAGNOSTIC_COMPLETENESS.md` ┬з7 for the complete list;
- that R13's four-class completeness contradicts `CONFLICT_RESOLUTION_TRILEMMA.md`'s own ┬з10 disclaimer that R11's seven named resolver *shapes* are not exhaustive. They are different, non-contradicting claims at different levels: R13 classifies structural *shapes* of diagnostic (four of them, closed by the Coq type), not an exhaustive enumeration of every function that could populate the scalar or structured class.
- that R14's `REFUSE-NO-COMPOSITE` is derived from `CoupledParallelCompatibility.v`'s `interface_disagreement_blocks_glue`/`interface_agreement_allows_glue`. It is not тАФ that theorem concerns a specific `Key -> option Value` glue construction, a related but distinct object from `ConflictDiagnostic V C`. R14 grounds the rule only in R13's own already-stated correspondence between `RefuseDiagnostic` and `interface_conflict`, not in a new derivation from the glue theorems;
- that R14 models time, concurrency, or multiple rounds of diagnostic refinement тАФ `RefinesByEvidence` is a single, one-shot `unresolved --> d` transition, not a general revision calculus, and nothing in R14 says when (or whether) evidence for an unresolved case ever arrives;
- that R14 commits `veribound-fce` to a stateful diagnostic-refinement engine. `NonLossyConflictDiagnostic` and `conflict_diagnostic_classifier.py` remain exactly what they are тАФ static schema and classifier. Whether R14's reduction rule ever gets an applied counterpart is a separate, unstarted question.
- that R15's `LocalConflict`/gluing theory generalises beyond the two-branch, one-seam domain `CoupledParallelCompatibility.v` formalises. Multi-region composition may carry cycle-level obstructions invisible at any single shared key тАФ see R1-R9's cocycle/coboundary machinery, a genuinely different obstruction theory not reducible to pairwise key comparison;
- that R15 supplies a deciding algorithm for `Compatible dA dB`. The proved equivalence `Compatible dA dB <-> exists g, IsGlue dA dB g` is a semantic fact, not a decision procedure тАФ `DecisivePairwiseEvidence dA dB` is never claimed to be constructible for every `dA dB`, and no such exhaustiveness theorem is attempted;
- that R15 addresses policy obstruction, global coherence, external authority rejection, or any executable certificate field. `PolicyObstruction`-style rejection has no theory anywhere in either repository; `fce_check.py` is untouched.
- that R16 proves `H^1` nontriviality anywhere. `nonzero_pairing_blocks_repair_mod_ceq` proves a residue is not a coboundary; it does not require or establish that the residue is a cocycle (`delta1 r = 0`), which a genuine nontrivial-cohomology-class claim would need in addition. Every obstruction fact R16 proves is named non-repairable, never nontrivial cohomology class;
- that R16 generalises `coboundaries_pair_zero` beyond whatever concrete `delta0`/`pairing`/`cycle` a caller supplies. That obligation is left to the instantiation тАФ the existing four-cycle (already proved) or a new general cover (a new, likely short, proof not attempted here) тАФ the same shape of per-domain obligation R15 left to `CoupledParallelCompatibility.v`'s own instantiation;
